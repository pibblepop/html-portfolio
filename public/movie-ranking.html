The Best Movies According to Tyler

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Harlem Nights

This is my all-time favorite comedy classic. Timeless stars and the plot make this movie great.

Stripes

This is my favorite because its the one that my dad and I watched together the most. Bill Murray is hilarious in it too.

X

This is my favorite because its the last movie I went to see in a theater with my mom. Also, Denzell Washington's portrayal of Malcom X makes it a classic.
